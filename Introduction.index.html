Welcome to the page 'The Korean foods that you must try!'. In this page, I introduce the Korean food that you might like to try!

Some Korean foods are difficult to try for their smell, texture and appearance, so I recommend foods that everyone would be satisfied with.

Explore our site to learn more about:

Bulgogi: Korean beef barbecue, is undoubtedly one of the most quintessentially Korean dishes there are: gorgeously thin slices of ribeye, tenderloin, or sirloin marinated in a savory-sweet sauce and quickly cooked over flame.

Samgye-tang: Ginseng chicken soup! Hot, steaming, delicious dish that features a small chicken stuffed with rice, ginseng, garlic, and jujube.

Dakgalbi: A spicy stir-fried chicken dish made with boneless chicken pieces, rice cakes (tteokbokki tteok), green cabbage, sweet potato, and other vegetables.
